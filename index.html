What is Blobbed

Storage without masters.

Traditional cloud storage — Google Drive, Dropbox, MEGA — stores your files on centralized servers. They can suspend your account. Inspect your data. Or disappear overnight.

Blobbed is different. Your files are encrypted in your browser before upload, then stored across a decentralized network of Shelby Protocol nodes. No single entity controls your data. No backdoors. No compromises.

How it works

Three steps. Total control.

01. Drop a file. It is instantly encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key that never leaves your device.

02. The encrypted blob is registered on Aptos and stored across Shelby storage providers — decentralized, redundant, always online.

03. Share with a link. The recipient downloads directly from Shelby nodes and decrypts in their browser. No servers in between. No trust required.
