FAQ

Questions answered.

Can Blobbed read my files?

No. Files are encrypted in your browser before upload. The decryption key never leaves your device.

What if Blobbed shuts down?

Your files live on Shelby Protocol nodes. Even if this website disappears, anyone with your link can download.

Is there a file size limit?

Currently ~100MB per file for the MVP. Shelby Protocol supports much larger blobs.

Why does upload require a wallet?

Registering a blob requires an on-chain transaction on Aptos. Downloading is free and anonymous.

How do I share a file?

After upload, click "Copy Link". The link contains blob hash and key in the URL fragment.
